# # Streamer Timer Setup Guide

## Step 1 – Open Streamlabs / OBS
Launch your streaming software.

---

## Step 2 – Add New Source
Click the + button in Sources.

---

## Step 3 – Select "Window Capture"
Choose Window Capture.

---

## Step 4 – Name the Source
Name it:
Streamer Timer

---

## Step 5 – Select the Timer Window
Choose:
CAPTURE THIS WINDOW

---

## Step 6 – Capture Settings
Recommended:
- Capture Method: Windows 10 (1903 and up)
- Match Priority: Match title
- Client Area: Enabled

---

## Step 7 – Transparency (IMPORTANT)
You will see a pink/magenta background.

This is intentional.

---

## Step 8 – Remove Background
In OBS:
- Add Color Key filter
- Set color to magenta (#FF00FF)

---

## Step 9 – Final Result
Timer should now appear clean over your stream.

---

## Notes
- Keep the capture window visible
- Works best with Window Capture
- Designed for Windows
